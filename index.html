About Us

A family-run business specializing in the building and maintaining of in-home aviaries for Colombian Chachalacas since 1992, we have only recently spread our wings into the highly sought-after market of in-home piranha aquariums.

Our business started with a vision- a vision which we have never regretted since the day of its inception- a vision to bring obscure species of South American wildlife into homes around the world.

The vision for this company was based in memory of Guillermo Vladimir Harrell VII, who visited the jungles of southwestern Colombia in the summer of 1990, and was amazed by the biodiversity of the region.  Before his death the following summer, he requested that the family continue his work studying techniques of smuggling birds across international borders. Thus began the saga of the Harrell Chachalaca smuggling operation, which has since grown into a business with tens of dollars of revenue.
